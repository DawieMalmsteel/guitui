SYMBOLS USED (Standard Tab Notation):
b  = bend (7b9 = bend 7 to pitch of 9)
r  = release (9r7 = release from 9 to 7)
/  = slide up (5/7 = slide from 5 to 7)
\  = slide down (7\5 = slide from 7 to 5)
h  = hammer-on (5h7)
p  = pull-off (7p5)
~  = vibrato (5~, 7~~)
l  = trill (5l7)
t  = tap (12t)
*  = pinch harmonic (7*)
<> = natural harmonic (<12>)

PICKING:
d  = down stroke
u  = up stroke
or
∏  = down (if terminal supports)
V  = up
